Frequently Asked Questions

How do I increase my credit limit?

You can request a credit limit increase through the mobile app or by calling customer service after 6 months of account opening.

What should I do if my card is declined?

Check your available credit, ensure your card is activated, and verify the transaction details. Contact support if the issue persists.

How long does it take to receive my card?

New cards are typically delivered within 7-10 business days after approval. Expedited shipping is available for premium members.
